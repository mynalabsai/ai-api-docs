LipSyncApi

All URIs are relative to http://gateway.neiro.ai

Method HTTP request Description
lipSyncContentLipSync POST /v1/content-lipsync Get video where someone says your text. Has a lot of configuration options.
lipSyncCustomVideo POST /v1/custom-video Clip your video with given audio and do lipsync
lipSyncGreeting POST /v1/greeting Get video with greeting for name.
lipSyncLipSync POST /v1/lipsync Get video with custom speech.
lipSyncLipSyncLooped POST /v1/lipsync-looped Get video with custom speech. End of video can be concatenated with initial template
lipSyncTextToTemplatedVideo POST /v1/text-to-templated-video Get video where someone says your text. Standart Frontend request.
lipSyncTextToTemplatedVideoSOTA POST /v1/text-to-templated-video-sota Get video where someone says your text. More accurate model with precalculations
