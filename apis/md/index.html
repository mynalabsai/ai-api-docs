Documentation for Neiro.ai ML Backend Gateway

Documentation for API Endpoints

All URIs are relative to http://gateway.neiro.ai

Class Method HTTP request Description
ASRApi aSRASR POST /v1/asr Extract text from audio.
DemosApi demosRandomPrediction POST /v1/random-prediction Get a random video from template and do text substitution.
LipSyncApi lipSyncContentLipSync POST /v1/content-lipsync Get video where someone says your text. Has a lot of configuration options.
LipSyncApi lipSyncCustomVideo POST /v1/custom-video Clip your video with given audio and do lipsync
LipSyncApi lipSyncGreeting POST /v1/greeting Get video with greeting for name.
LipSyncApi lipSyncLipSync POST /v1/lipsync Get video with custom speech.
LipSyncApi lipSyncLipSyncLooped POST /v1/lipsync-looped Get video with custom speech. End of video can be concatenated with initial template
LipSyncApi lipSyncTextToTemplatedVideo POST /v1/text-to-templated-video Get video where someone says your text. Standart Frontend request.
LipSyncApi lipSyncTextToTemplatedVideoSOTA POST /v1/text-to-templated-video-sota Get video where someone says your text. More accurate model with precalculations
TTSApi tTSNewTTS POST /v1/new-tts Get an audio from text.
TTSApi tTSTTS POST /v1/tts Get an audio from text.
TTSApi tTSTTSV2 POST /v2/tts Get an audio from text.
TTSApi tTSTTSV3 POST /v3/tts Get an audio from text.
VCApi vCNewVC POST /v1/new-vc Get audio with another voice.
VCApi vCVC POST /v1/vc Get audio with another voice.
VCApi vCVCV2 POST /v2/vc Get audio with another voice.
VCApi vCVCV3 POST /v3/vc Get audio with another voice.
